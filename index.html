    Available in light and dark

    Our template is readily available in both light and dark theme. You can easily switch between the two by using the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + Alt + T or execute base.theme.change() with a button. Like this one:

    Change Theme

    Pre-made building components

    Our web template is equipped with pre-made building components and helper classes to give you an easy building experience. We simplify web creation to make it accessible to everyone, even for those who may not have technical expertise. No lengthy manuals or confusing rules -- just dive in and start building. We have also prepared some demo articles to help you out.
